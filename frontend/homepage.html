Purpose

Texting is a very opaque medium, and it is difficult to interpret emotional state from the way a person types. But in conversations with friends, family, and important people it is especially important to understand how the other interlocutor feels in a given interaction. What Do You Mean (WDYM) aims to clarify these feelings using sentiment analysis.
